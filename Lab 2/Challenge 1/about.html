Who is this Author anyway?

Colin Buchanan is a Graduate Apprentice, studying Cybersecurity in the School of Computing at Edinburgh Napier University. Most of his time is spent procrastinating on work, then hurriedly completing the tasks, and struggling with imposter syndrome.

Colin was born in Edinburgh on 25/11/1997, at 11:32pm. He lived for the majority of his life in and around Thurso, the most northern major town in the Highlands, and attended High School and College there. He has since moved to Stirling, has a partner who he adores, and a dog who is adored just as much by both.
